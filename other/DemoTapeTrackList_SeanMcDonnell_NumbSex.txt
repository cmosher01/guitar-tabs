band: Numb Sex
lead singer: Sean McDonnell

I met Sean when we were freshmen at Syracuse University in the Fall of 1984. He was from Cheshire, Connecticut. We were both into Punk Rock, and he told me about his band "Numb Sex". He gave me one of the band's recent demo cassette tapes. This is the track listing:

1. Target for Demand
2. Wash Your Hands
3. Interference
4. Habit
5. Herpes
6. Snuffed
7. Another Fake Jesus/Suburbia's Scared (Mock Thrash Dub)
8. Chinese Rocks
